Our Journey

Founded in 2010 by master craftsman John Laety, our workshop began as a small garage operation with a simple mission: to create furniture that tells a story. Today, we're a team of 15 passionate artisans dedicated to preserving traditional woodworking techniques while embracing innovative designs.
